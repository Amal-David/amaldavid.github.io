Pocket Chat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Pocket Chat is a simple browser extension that opens a chat assistant in a small, floating window so you can keep it handy while you work.

What we collect

Nothing that leaves your device. The only thing Pocket Chat stores is the size and position of its own window, saved locally in your browser using the Chrome storage API, so the window reopens where you left it. This stays on your computer.

What we do not do

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, browsing history, message content, or analytics. Pocket Chat has no servers and makes no network requests of its own.

Third-party sites

Pocket Chat opens third-party websites (the chat assistant you choose) inside a window. Your use of those sites is governed by their own privacy policies, not this one.
